Overview

The EVA Data Portal provides users the ability to dynamically query EVA variants which are displayed at the variant, transcript, gene or genome level via our visualisation

New features will be regularly added by the EVA development team. Please send all comments and feedback to eva-helpdesk@ebi.ac.uk

  Information

Download Data : All of our data is open-access and can be downloaded

Submit: EVA welcomes direct submission of all types of genetic variation from all species
